About Us

Jubilee Community Church is a local church family committed to lifting up Jesus Christ, preaching the Word of God, and building believers who live with faith, holiness, and compassion.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples through biblical preaching, Spirit-led worship, prayer, and practical ministry that strengthens families and impacts our community.

What We Believe

  • Jesus Christ is Lord and Savior.
  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God and our foundation for faith and practice.
  • Salvation is found in Christ alone, producing a transformed life.
  • We value prayer, worship, discipleship, and service.
